RECIPE TO COOK CHICKEN CURRY

Ingredients

  1. 800g chicken
  2. 1/2-1 coconut milk
  3. 180g curry paste
  4. 3 large potato
  5. 3 large onion
  6. 3 stalks curry leaves

Cooking Step

Proses Cooking Step
1 First, saute curry paste in hot oil until fragrant, add in chicken, then continue to saute until chicken releases oil.
2 Second, add in water, tamarind paste, curry leaves, potato, onion, sugar and salt.
Let chicken simmer in slow fire until potatoes becomes soft and chicken is cooked thoroughly.
3 Lastly, add in coconut milk and cook until oil starts to separate.
Remove and serve.
