DETAIL OF PSYCOLOGY COURSE

MODULES
Year 1

Core modules include :
  • The Person and the Brain
  • Learning about Learning
  • Introduction to Psychological Research
  • Debates in Mental Health
  • Applied & Professional Psychology
  • Psychology in the Workplace
Year 2

Core modules include :
  • Cognitive and Biological Psychology
  • Development Across the Lifespan
  • Research Methods in Psychology
  • Social Psychology and Individual Differences
  • Professional and Clinical Practice ( with work placement )
Optional modules include
  • Alternative Dispute Resolution
  • Behaviour Change
  • Founder Dilemmas
Year 3

Core modules include :
  • Research Project
  • Communicating Current Psychology
  • Practicum / Industrial Training
Optional modules include
  • Counselling: Theory and Practice
  • Face Processing
  • Forensic Psychology
  • Psychology and Culture
  • Visual Perception : Physiology, Psychophysics, and Psychology

*Please note that all modules are subject to change.
CAREER PROSPECTS
Our psychology graduates typically enter a wide range of careers, including:
  • Clinical psychology and counselling
  • Education, health promotion and caring professions
  • Advertising, media, marketing and sales
  • Human resources, management and organisational psychology
ENTRY REQUIREMENTS
EITHER WITH
  1. International Foundation Programme:

    SPM, IGCSE and equivalent holders can enrol onto the International Foundation Programme as a pathway to this programme.

    OR

  2. Direct entry with STPM/A Levels* with grades: BBB

    *We also (subject to conditions) accept Unified Examination Certificate (UEC), International Baccalaureate (IB), Australian Matriculation and Exams, Canadian Pre-U and Exams,
    Internationally Recognised Diploma and Foundation and other relevant qualification accredited and recognised by the Malaysian Qualifications Agency (MQA).

    Kindly contact us for further details on the entry requirements.
**English Language Requirement:
  • Grade B or higher at SPM English 1119 / IGCSE English
  • IELTS: Band 6.5 with no component below 5.5 or equivalent.
**University English courses are available for students with English grade below B or IELTS 6.5
